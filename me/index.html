About

SgExamPapers is dedicated to making available to everyone the most recent examination and exercise papers administered in Singapore’s public schools. We only provide old examination papers and that is all we do. By staying true to this mission, we are:

  • Transparent: We show all papers by grade level, subject, year administrator and originating schools
  • Safe: Since we host all papers on github.com, all our files are publicly visible. There are no viruses or scams.
  • Fast: We require no emails or logins.
  • Free: There are no charges or hidden fees.

We do this as a public service for the Singapore public. We only ask that if you find this site useful, feel free to pass on this site to your friends and neighbors.

We welcome any feedback for improvements. Please email any comments or suggestions to sgexampapers2018 @ gmail.com.
